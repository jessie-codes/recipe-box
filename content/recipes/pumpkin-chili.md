## Ingredients

- 14oz canned pumpkin puree
- 14oz canned chili beans
- 14oz canned fire roasted diced tomatoes
- 14oz vegetable broth
- 1 (1-1.5oz) packet chili seasoning
- 1 medium yellow onion, diced

## Instructions

1. Add all ingredients to the base of a slow cooker. Stir to combine.
2. Cook on low for 8 hours, or high for 4 hours.

## Notes

Optionally serve with sour cream, shredded cheese, or corn bread.
