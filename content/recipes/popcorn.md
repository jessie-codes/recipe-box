## Ingredients

- 1/4 cup popcorn kernels
- 2 tbsp neutral oil or coconut oil
- 1-2 tbsp butter (optional)
- Salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Add oil and 2-3 popcorn kernels to a large pot with a lid.
2. Heat over medium heat until the test kernels pop.
3. Add remaining popcorn kernels and cover with lid.
4. Gently shake the pot occasionally while the popcorn cooks.
5. Once popping slows to several seconds between pops, remove from heat.
6. Add butter and salt, then toss to combine.

## Notes

Optional toppings include parmesan cheese, nutritional yeast, chili powder, ranch seasoning, or caramel drizzle.
