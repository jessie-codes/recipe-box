---
title: Cinnamon Roll
---

## Ingredients

### Dough
- 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 packet active dry yeast
- 1 cup warm milk
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1/3 cup melted butter
- 1 egg
- 1/2 tsp salt

### Filling
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 2 tbsp cinnamon
- 1/4 cup softened butter

### Icing
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 2 tbsp milk
- 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

---

## Instructions

1. Combine warm milk, yeast, and sugar in a bowl. Let sit for 5 minutes.
2. Mix in melted butter, egg, and salt.
3. Gradually add flour and knead until smooth.
4. Cover and let dough rise for 1 hour.
5. Roll dough into a rectangle and spread softened butter over it.
6. Sprinkle cinnamon and brown sugar evenly across the dough.
7. Roll tightly into a log and cut into rolls.
8. Place rolls in a greased baking dish and let rise for 30 minutes.
9. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 20–25 minutes.
10. Mix icing ingredients and drizzle over warm rolls.
