## Ingredients

- 3 8oz blocks cream cheese, soften
- 5 large eggs, room temperature
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350° F.
2. To a large mixing bowl, alternate adding one egg and one block of cream cheese. Mix until just combined. There will be lumps in the batter, do not overmix.
3. Add the sugar and vanilla extra and stir through.
4. Pour batter into a large glass or ceramic pie pan, or 9x9 baking dish. Do not use metal bakeware. Bake for 50 minutes. The middle should be jiggly and the top browned.
5. Let cool completely in the fridge, at least 4 hours. Waiting 24 hours before serving produces the best taste.

## Notes

This simple recipe relies on not overmixing to create the ideal texture. Use glass or ceramic bakeware. Metal pans will leave a metallic taste in the final product.
