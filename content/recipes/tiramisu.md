## Ingredients

- 16 oz Mascarpone cheese 
- 4 egg yolks- 2/3 cup granulated sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 30-36 ladyfingers
- 1 1/2 cup strong black coffee
- 2 tbsp cocoa powder to dust

## Instructions

1. Dunk the ladyfingers in the coffee
2. Layer in a pan
2. Chill 6 hours
